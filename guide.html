📖 Beginner Guide

⚔️ What is a Pokémon Battle?

In Pokémon Champions, trainers battle using teams of Pokémon. The goal is to defeat all of your opponent's Pokémon before they defeat yours.

🔥 Understanding Types

Every Pokémon has one or two types. Types affect which moves are strong or weak against other Pokémon.

  • Fire
  • Water
  • Grass
  • Electric
  • Dragon
  • And many more...

⭐ What is STAB?

STAB stands for Same Type Attack Bonus.

If a Pokémon uses a move that matches one of its types, the move becomes stronger.

Example: Charizard uses Flamethrower. Because Charizard is Fire-type and Flamethrower is a Fire-type move, Flamethrower gets a damage boost.

💪 Physical vs Special Moves

Physical moves use the Pokémon's Attack stat.

Special moves use the Pokémon's Special Attack stat.

Example:

  • Flamethrower → Special Move → Uses Special Attack
  • Fire Punch → Physical Move → Uses Attack

😴 Status Conditions

  • Burn - Reduces physical damage and deals damage each turn.
  • Poison - Deals damage every turn.
  • Sleep - Prevents actions for several turns.
  • Paralysis - Can stop movement and lowers Speed.
  • Freeze - Prevents movement until thawed.

🏆 Beginner Tips

  • Learn type matchups.
  • Don't use only attacking moves.
  • Switch Pokémon when needed.
  • Build a balanced team.
  • Practice and learn from your losses.
