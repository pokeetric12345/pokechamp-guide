📖 Beginner Guide

Welcome to PokéChamp Guide! If you're new to Pokémon Champions, start here.

⚔️ What is a Pokémon Battle?

In a Pokémon battle, each trainer uses Pokémon to defeat their opponent's team. The last trainer with Pokémon remaining wins.

🔥 Understanding Types

Every Pokémon has one or two types.

  • Fire
  • Water
  • Grass
  • Electric
  • Dragon
  • And many more...

Types determine which moves are strong or weak against other Pokémon.

⭐ What is STAB?

STAB stands for Same Type Attack Bonus.

If a Pokémon uses a move that matches one of its types, the move becomes stronger.

Example: Charizard uses Flamethrower. Since Charizard is Fire-type and Flamethrower is Fire-type, the move gets a power boost.

💪 Physical vs Special Moves

Physical Moves: Use the Attack stat.

Special Moves: Use the Special Attack stat.

Knowing which stat your Pokémon is better at helps you choose stronger moves.

😴 Status Conditions

  • Burn - Reduces physical damage.
  • Poison - Damages a Pokémon each turn.
  • Sleep - Prevents actions for several turns.
  • Paralysis - May stop movement and lowers speed.
  • Freeze - Prevents movement until thawed.

🏆 Beginner Tips

  • Don't use only attacking moves.
  • Switch Pokémon when needed.
  • Learn type matchups.
  • Build a balanced team.
  • Practice and learn from losses.
